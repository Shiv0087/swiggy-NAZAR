## рҹҺҜ The Problem

Every Indian household buys the same 10вҖ“15 grocery items on Instamart every week вҖ” atta, dal, chawal, tel, sugar. These prices change constantly. Swiggy runs a **Mega Savings Festival** (up to 60% off) in the first 3 days of every month. Most people miss it entirely.

> **The current workaround? A notebook.**
> 
> *"Swiggy does not have a price drop alert function вҖ” users have to keep track of prices manually, open the app, write down the price in a notebook."* вҖ” NewsBytesApp, Jan 2025

On the food side: Swiggy publishes **"How India Swiggy'd"** вҖ” a national annual report. Biryani is #1 (for 10 years running). That's interesting. But it tells you nothing about what's trending **in your neighbourhood, this week.**

**Nazar fixes both.**

## рҹ‘ҒпёҸ What Nazar Does

### 1 В· Instamart Price Watcher

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Price History** | Tracks prices of your past-ordered Instamart items over 30 / 60 / 90 days вҖ” visualised as a chart |
| **Drop Alerts** | WhatsApp + push notification the moment a tracked item falls below your set price |
| **Smart Buy Timing** | Learns Instamart's Mega Sale pattern (Day 1вҖ“3 every month) and reminds you 24hrs before it starts |
| **My Watchlist** | Save your 8вҖ“15 regular items (atta, dal, oil, sugar) вҖ” track all in one dashboard |
| **Cross-Platform Compare** | Price history for the same item on Instamart vs Blinkit vs Zepto вҖ” best deal over time, not just today |
| **One-Tap Order** | Alert fires вҶ’ user taps вҶ’ Instamart cart opens with that item pre-added |

### 2 В· Hyperlocal Trend Finder

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Neighbourhood Trends** | "In your area this week: Pav Bhaji orders вҶ‘34%, new cloud kitchen trending" |
| **Rising Dishes** | Dishes gaining order volume fast in your pin code вҖ” before everyone else discovers them |
| **New Restaurant Radar** | Restaurants that opened recently in your area AND are already gaining traction |
| **Weekly Digest** | Every Monday morning: a 5-line WhatsApp summary of what's hot near you this week |
| **AI Trend Summaries** | Claude reads raw order signals and writes human-readable trend cards вҖ” not just numbers |

## рҹ”Ң Swiggy MCP Integration

| MCP Server | Tool Used | Powers |
|---|---|---|
| **Swiggy Instamart** | `search_products` | Find user's tracked items by name + variant |
| **Swiggy Instamart** | `get_product_details` | Fetch current price, MRP, discount % |
| **Swiggy Food** | `search_restaurants` | Neighbourhood restaurant discovery |
| **Swiggy Food** | `get_restaurant_menu` | Dish-level data for trend tracking |
| **Swiggy Dineout** | `search_restaurants` | Dineout venue trends by area |

**Auth flow:** OAuth 2.0 via Swiggy MCP вҖ” user connects their Swiggy account once, Nazar accesses their order history to auto-populate the watchlist. No manual item entry needed.

## рҹ’Ў Why This Doesn't Exist Yet

| Existing Tool | What it does | Why it's not Nazar |
|---|---|---|
| **MetricsCart / 42Signals** | Tracks Instamart prices for FMCG brands | B2B only вҖ” for Amul, HUL tracking their own SKUs. Costs вӮ№lakhs/month. Not for consumers. |
| **Savvio / Quick Compare** | Instamart vs Blinkit price today | Point-in-time only. No history. No alerts. No buy timing. |
| **Keepa / Camelizer** | Amazon India price history + alerts | Excellent product вҖ” but zero Instamart support. Groceries aren't on Amazon. |
| **Swiggy app itself** | Everything | Zero price tracking, zero watchlist, zero alerts. Confirmed missing feature. |
| **Zomato Trends** | Restaurant supply-demand data | For restaurant owners making menu decisions. Not consumer-facing. National scale only. |
| **Swiggy annual report** | "93M biryanis ordered in India" | National, annual. Not your neighbourhood, not this week. |

**The gap:** No product tracks MY grocery prices over time on Instamart, alerts me when they drop, and shows me what's trending near me this week. Nazar is the first.
